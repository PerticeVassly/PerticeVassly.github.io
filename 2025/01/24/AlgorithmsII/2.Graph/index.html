Breadth-first search

also find path froms s to t that uses fewest number of edges

Put s onto a FIFO queue, and marked s as visited

Repeat until the queue is empty:

  • remove the least recently added vertex v

  • add each of v’s unvisited neighbors to the queue and marked them as visited

Proof: Queue always consists pf zero or more vertices of distance k from s followed by zero or more vertices of distance k + 1

Proof: Each vertex connected to s is visited exactly once
